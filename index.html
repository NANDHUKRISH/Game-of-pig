Game of PIG

Instructions

The game of Pig is a very simple jeopardy dice game in which two players race to reach 50 points. Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled or the player holds and scores the sum of the rolls (i.e. the turn total). At any time during a player's turn, the player is faced with two decisions:

  1. ROLL DICE -If the player rolls a
    • 1: the player scores nothing and it becomes the opponent's turn.
    • 2 - 6: the number is added to the player's Current total and the player's turn continues.
  2. HOLD - The current total is added to the player's score and it becomes the opponent's turn.

There are two players . The violet box indicates the current player. Who First scores 50 points will win .

"Once a Player has won , He will be highlighted as winner and then Press the Button "NEW GAME" to start a New Game "
